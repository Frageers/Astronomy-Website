Exploring the universe,
one week at a time

Astronomy club is a place where students can come to learn about different aspects of astronomy and how they can use astronomy to branch out to other branches of science, including but not limited to, astrophysics, aerospace engineering, and astronomer. Through multiple engaging activities throughout the year, such as an astronomy themed escape room, or an astronomy themed wellness day session, Astronomy club aims to foster a sense of curiosity and to provide and equip students with the information and tools they need to possibly pursue Astronomy as a career or a hobby.
